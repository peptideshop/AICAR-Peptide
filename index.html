AICAR Peptide Research Applications

What laboratory research has explored with AICAR.

AICAR is one of the most widely cited AMPK activators in published metabolic research literature.

01 / AMPK Activation

Mechanism of Action

AICAR is metabolized intracellularly into ZMP, an AMP analog that activates AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) — a central regulator of cellular energy balance studied extensively in vitro and in animal models.

02 / Metabolic Research

Lipid & Glucose Pathway Studies

Published research has used AICAR to investigate AMPK's downstream role in fatty acid oxidation and glucose uptake pathways in cultured cells and rodent models — foundational work in metabolic disease research.

03 / Exercise Physiology Models

"Exercise Mimetic" Literature

AICAR has been studied as a tool compound for probing exercise-associated gene expression changes in skeletal muscle tissue, particularly in preclinical models exploring endurance-adaptation pathways.
